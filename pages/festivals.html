Dashain

Dashain is the greatest and most celebreated festival in the context of Nepal. Nepal is the country of 80% Hindu people and it is celebrated by Hindu peoles. In Dashain, all the people get together and celebrate with tika, blessings and great feasts.

Tihar

Tihar is the most interesting festival in Nepal because all the houses are decorated with flowers and lights. In tiher, dogs, crows, cows and oxes are worshipped. On the last day of Tihar, every sister worships his brothers for long life.
